Planet Overview

Name: Nerthys

Type: Oceanic planet

Size: Slightly smaller than Earth

Gravity: 0.85g

Distance from Parent Star: Farther than Earth, receiving minimal sunlight

Atmosphere: Dense with thick clouds, mainly methane, hydrogen sulfide, CO2, and nitrogen

Surface Temperature: -20°C

Energy Source: Geothermal activity (hydrothermal vents)

Ecosystem Zones of Nerthys

Hydrothermal Vent Gardens

Life thrives in the heat and chemicals released by hydrothermal vents.

Mid-Ocean Plains

Vast plains populated by unique grazers and filter feeders.

Methane Seep Fields

Rich fields where methane supports chemosynthetic life.

Twilight Rift Valleys

Dark and mysterious trenches where massive creatures like Titan Squids roam.

Species of Nerthys

Giant Worms

Large, segmented creatures with symbiotic bacteria.

Glass Serpents

Transparent eels that glide through the dark waters, hunting smaller creatures.

Whisker Eels

Ribbon-like eels that sense chemicals with their whiskers, detecting distant food sources.

Unique Adaptations on Nerthys

  • Bioluminescence Communication: Used for hunting and communication in dark waters.
  • Metal-Infused Exoskeletons: Provides protection against predators in a high-pressure environment.
  • Chemical Sensory Systems: Advanced chemical detectors help creatures sense food and danger in pitch-black water.
